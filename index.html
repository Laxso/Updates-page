Progress Update 0.1

This is where I’ll be sharing occasional updates on the game. As much as I’d love to release it by winter, that’s no longer realistic. We’ve decided to push things back a bit. Everyone has been working incredibly hard, and I gave the team the summer off to recharge. The project is still moving forward and very much possible with our current team, but since we’re missing a few people with certain skill sets, progress will naturally take a little longer.

As for the script, we’re sitting at 65,248 words (about 271 pages). It’s already been through heavy editing, but of course it will need another round before the final release. We’re moving into Chapter 15 next, though some chapters are much longer than others (looking at you, Chapter 14). In fact, that one will likely be split into four chapters once the game comes out.
